About us

We are brewers. We brew great beers and other beverages that our consumers across the country love. Every brand from our stable holds the promise to deliver to your satisfaction.
Our brew masters make this happen. Talented and eminently skilled in the art and science of brewing, they painstakingly source the best ingredients for your brands. They do not stop there. They preserve old recipes and never cease to invent new ones. This is why we confidently pledge to continue to brew the brands you love, for decades to come. Our brew masters, brewers, technologists, technicians, scientists and others operate out of four breweries located in different parts of Nigeria.
